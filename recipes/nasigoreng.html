Nasi Goreng Recipe

Description

Nasi Goreng is Indonesia’s iconic fried rice dish, known for its rich, smoky flavor and a touch of sweetness from kecap manis (sweet soy sauce). It’s often served with a fried egg, crackers, and pickles on the side.

Ingredients

  • 2 plates of cold cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 shallot, sliced
  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chili sauce (optional)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Fried shallots and sliced cucumber for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok or pan over medium heat
  2. Sauté garlic and shallot until fragrant
  3. Push them aside and scramble the eggs in the same pan
  4. Add cold rice and stir well to combine
  5. Season with sweet soy sauce, soy sauce, chili sauce, salt, and pepper
  6. Stir-fry until rice is evenly coated and slightly caramelized
  7. Serve hot, topped with fried shallots and a sunny-side-up egg
  8. Garnish with sliced cucumber and tomato if desired

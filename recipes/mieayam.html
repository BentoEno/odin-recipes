Mie Ayam Recipe

Description

Mie Ayam is a popular Indonesian noodle dish made with springy egg noodles topped with seasoned chicken, mushrooms, and a savory soy-based sauce. It’s often served with a side of chicken broth and garnished with bok choy and fried shallots.

Ingredients

Chicken Topping:

  • 300 g chicken thigh or breast, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • ½ tsp sesame oil (optional)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp white pepper
  • 100 ml water or chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Noodles:

  • 250 g fresh egg noodles
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil or chicken fat
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp sesame oil
  • Salt to taste

Broth:

  • 500 ml chicken stock
  • 1 clove garlic, smashed
  • A pinch of salt and pepper

Topping:

  • Blanched bok choy or mustard greens
  • Fried shallots
  • Sliced scallions
  • Chili sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil and sauté garlic until fragrant
  2. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink
  3. Add all sauces, sugar, pepper, and water. Simmer until the sauce thickens
  4. In another pot, boil chicken stock with garlic, salt, and pepper for 10 minutes
  5. Boil noodles for 2–3 minutes, drain, and toss with oil, soy sauce, sesame oil, and salt
  6. Arrange noodles in a bowl and top with cooked chicken
  7. Add bok choy, fried shallots, and scallions
  8. Serve with a bowl of warm broth and optional chili sauce
